Solo leveling

Origin: Based on the hit Korean web novel/webtoon Solo Leveling (Na Honjaman Level Up). Story: Follows Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter in a world filled with monsters and dungeons. After a mysterious event, he gains the power to level up infinitely, becoming humanity’s strongest hunter.

solo Leveling villan

The true power of a villain in Solo Leveling lies not just in strength, but in the fear they inspire. The Monarchs, with their godlike abilities, see humans as mere insects, crushing them without hesitation. Among them, the Dragon Monarch, Antares, stands as the ultimate enemy—cold, ruthless, and nearly unstoppable. His presence alone reminds the world that even the strongest heroes can tremble before true darkness.



  • Ant King – Deadly boss monster from the Jeju Island Arc, nearly wipes out entire teams of hunters.
  • Hwang Dong-Su – Arrogant S-rank hunter, driven by revenge against Jin-Woo.
  • Beast Monarch – Brutal and savage Monarch, thrives on raw strength and destruction.
  • Frost Monarch – Calm but merciless, wields devastating ice powers.
